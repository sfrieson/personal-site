Education

Machine Learning

As a fun exploration as well possible professional development, I took a course in machine learning.

Career

Aside from part-time jobs including teaching piano, working in fast food, and working at a yarn store, I've mainly had two careers.

Design

Coming out of college my first couple of jobs were in graphic design. Working for New York Cares, I was able to work on some amazing projects like putting advertisements on Times Square billboards for the annual Coat Drive.

Development

Since finishing GA, I've had a few tech jobs. The focus of each job has been very different, but the front end technologies at each have centered around React.

RW3

At my first tech job, I was the sole front end developer, and was charged with the task of implementing the redesign of their flagship e-learning software CultureWizard.

TheaterMania

My next experience was a similar one. I joined their small team at the beginning of a redesign of their website, which publishes theater news and sells tickets. At TheaterMania, I gained experience in a few backend technologies like GraphQL and ElasticSearch, as well as deepening my front end skills with performance, SEO, and ad service.

CB Insights

My current job has been a big change for me, in that it's the first time I've worked on a medium-sized engineering team and on enterprise software in the business/finance sector. I've worked on feature teams, but also championed multiple successful efforts around code quality and developer experience.
